    • ALICE IN HASSLE LAND

    Alice has always been a pretentious young woman, impertinent but always perfect on her.
    She is very obstinate, she believes (knows) to be right and often allows reflections and other ease
    as unwelcome as disturbing. Not displeasing to his entourage; this one knows how to take revenge.

    Creation of a book about the famous novel by Lewis Carroll. The plastic realization of the pages was
    made with weft constituting the grain paper, mixed with the layer.
