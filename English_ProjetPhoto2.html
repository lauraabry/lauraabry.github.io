    • APPEAR IN THE CROWD
    2017


    Set up and shooting direction for the realization of the trombinoscope around the identities
    and singularities of the students of BTS and DSAA in Digital Graphic Design.
    The idea was to stage a crowd in perpetual movement, which would gradually dissipate revealing
    the singular student.
