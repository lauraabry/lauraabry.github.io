WHO I AM ?


Me, Laura Abry. I'm 21 yo and I'm a student in Digital Graphic Design
in Boulogne Billancourt, in the suburbs of Paris.
I am a great passionate of print, very admiring the work
of Michel Wlassikoff, Lazar Lissitzy, Roman Cieslewicz and Anh Sang Soo.
I'm also music lover and guitar player in my lost hours.
Auditor of leeway music, I share the leitmotiv of the french rapper Espiiem
since I "meets many circles without being of any medium."
Les Sages Poetes de La Rue and Mc Solaar et Le Gouffre are for me the engines of
French rap, but I will never leave the KEPX radio, les Doors, Herbie Hancock,
Miles Davis with whom I grew up.
Don't forget Detroit's techno !
Besides, if you must remember a name, remember Underground Resistance.
The cinema is always at the rendez-vous when its comes to develop my curiosity
and I am undoubtedly a big partisan of Lars Von Trier's cinema.
Skiing and skateboarding have made me an adventurous person but also
an intrepid person; who discovers and travels all over the world ; from Burma,
China and Malaysia to Hungary via the greats Americans coasts.
